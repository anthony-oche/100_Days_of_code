Rochi's Favourite Series of all Time

Top 3 Below

Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones achieved massive popularity and is considered by many to be an all-time favorite series due to its cinematic scale on television, complex, morally gray characters, unpredictable and intricate plot, and ability to generate an immense, shared global cultural conversation.

Prison Break

Prison Break is a favorite for its initial gripping premise, driven by Michael Scofield's genius plan to save his brother, intense suspense, constant cliffhangers, and themes of loyalty and family, keeping viewers hooked with intricate puzzles and high stakes, especially in the iconic first season. While later seasons sometimes stretched believability with elaborate conspiracies, the show's addictive nature, compelling characters (like the complex T-Bag), unique prison setting, and strong emotional core cemented its status as a beloved, thrilling escape saga for many fans.

Night Action

The Night Agent is not universally considered the "favourite series of all time," but it became one of Netflix's most-watched shows ever due to its effective blend of action, political conspiracy, and binge-worthy structure.
